🎉 THE EVENT PLANNERS

"With You Through Every Chapter."

At Olivia & Ava PrimePlanners, we believe that every moment—whether filled with joy or touched by sorrow—deserves to be honored with care, compassion, and excellence. Based in Hobart, Tasmania, and proudly serving the surrounding areas, we specialize in creating seamless, meaningful experiences for life’s most significant events.

From vibrant weddings and milestone celebrations to respectful memorials and farewells, our dedicated team is here to support you every step of the way. We combine emotional sensitivity with cutting-edge technology to deliver unforgettable results. Our services feature high-resolution cameras, aerial drone coverage, dynamic display systems, crystal-clear sound setups, and a multi-talented crew that brings creativity and professionalism to every event.

Behind the scenes, our responsive and efficient logistics team ensures that everything runs smoothly—on time, every time. Whether it's a joyous gathering or a solemn tribute, we’re committed to delivering personalized, high-quality experiences that truly matter.

Our mission is simple: to walk beside you during life’s most meaningful chapters, taking the pressure off and allowing you to be fully present. We specialise in creating events that feel personal, authentic, and beautifully planned — down to the smallest details.

Whether you're welcoming a new life, marking a milestone, or honouring a loved one’s memory, we’re here to guide you every step of the way.

  • Thoughtfully tailored services for both joyful and sorrowful events
  • Local knowledge of venues, vendors, and cultural traditions in Hobart and beyond
  • Warm, compassionate, and professional planning team
  • A calm presence during life’s most emotional moments
