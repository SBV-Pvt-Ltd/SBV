🧠 Head

The head contains your brain, which controls your entire body! It's protected by your skull bone.

🤔 Fun Fact!

Your brain weighs about 3 pounds and uses 20% of your body's energy!

👁️ Eyes

Your eyes help you see the world around you! They can detect millions of colors and work like cameras.

✨ Fun Fact!

You blink about 15-20 times per minute to keep your eyes moist!

👃 Nose

Your nose helps you smell and breathe. It can detect over 1 trillion different scents!

🌸 Fun Fact!

Your nose also helps you taste food - that's why food tastes different when you have a cold!

👄 Mouth

Your mouth is used for eating, speaking, and breathing. It contains your teeth and tongue!

🦷 Fun Fact!

Adults have 32 teeth, and your mouth produces about 1 liter of saliva every day!

👂 Ears

Your ears help you hear sounds and also help you keep your balance!

🎵 Fun Fact!

Your ears never stop working - they even hear sounds while you sleep!

🦒 Neck

Your neck supports your head and allows you to turn it in different directions.

💪 Fun Fact!

Your neck has 7 vertebrae (neck bones) - the same number as a giraffe!

🤷 Shoulders

Your shoulders connect your arms to your torso and allow you to move your arms in many directions - up, down, forward, backward, and in circles!

🔄 Fun Fact!

Your shoulder is a ball-and-socket joint that can move in more directions than almost any other joint in your body!

🫁 Chest

Your chest contains your heart and lungs. Your heart pumps blood and your lungs help you breathe!

💓 Fun Fact!

Your heart beats about 100,000 times per day!

💪 Upper Arm

The upper arm connects your shoulder to your elbow. It contains strong muscles like the biceps and triceps that help you lift and move things!

🦴 Fun Fact!

The upper arm bone (humerus) is the longest bone in your arm!

🤲 Elbow

Your elbow is a joint that lets you bend and straighten your arm. It connects your upper arm to your lower arm!

⚡ Fun Fact!

When you hit your "funny bone," you're actually hitting a nerve that runs near your elbow!

🦾 Lower Arm

Your lower arm goes from your elbow to your wrist. It has two bones that help you twist your arm and move your hand!

🔄 Fun Fact!

The two bones in your lower arm can rotate around each other to let you turn your palm up and down!

✋ Hands

Your hands are amazing tools! They can write, draw, play instruments, and feel textures.

👆 Fun Fact!

Each hand has 27 bones and over 30 muscles!

🫄 Stomach

Your stomach digests the food you eat, breaking it down so your body can use it for energy!

🍎 Fun Fact!

Your stomach can stretch to hold about 1.5 liters of food!

🦵 Thigh

Your thigh is the upper part of your leg, from your hip to your knee. It contains the strongest muscle in your body - the quadriceps!

💪 Fun Fact!

Your thigh bone (femur) is the longest and strongest bone in your entire body!

🦵 Knee

Your knee is a hinge joint that lets you bend and straighten your leg. It helps you walk, run, jump, and sit down!

🦴 Fun Fact!

Your kneecap (patella) is actually a bone that sits in front of your knee joint to protect it!

🦵 Lower Leg

Your lower leg goes from your knee to your ankle. It has two bones that help support your weight and let you move your foot!

🏃 Fun Fact!

Your calf muscles in your lower leg help you push off the ground when you walk or run!

🦶 Feet

Your feet help you balance and move around. They have many small bones and muscles working together!

👣 Fun Fact!

Each foot has 26 bones and 33 joints!
