About Us



Mr. Saharsh Bhadani

(Director and Promoter)




Dr. Harshit Bhadani

(Director)


Indian Prime Minister once famously remarked, "Data is the new oil," emphasizing the growing significance of data in today's world. Much like how oil, and by extension energy, gives companies in the petroleum and petrochemical industries a competitive advantage, data now serves as a crucial resource for modern businesses. Just as energy drives growth in traditional sectors, data empowers businesses to leverage lean principles, streamline operations, and enhance decision-making. This, in turn, enables the adoption of more sustainable and efficient business practices, setting the stage for long-term success in an increasingly data-driven economy.
At Saharsh Bhadani Venture Private Limited, we view our clients as true partners, and our team is equipped with the expertise and tools necessary to harness and analyze the data generated by our partner companies. By transforming this data into actionable insights, we empower our partners to optimize their operations and achieve maximum efficiency. Our goal is to help our partners gain a competitive edge, while simultaneously nurturing and strengthening their business ecosystems. We firmly believe in the power of a symbiotic ecosystem, where the collective growth and success of all stakeholders drives our own growth, creating a cycle of continuous improvement and shared prosperity.


Our Location


The company is strategically situated in the vibrant metropolitan city of Kolkata, West Bengal, India. As one of the country's most prominent urban centers, Kolkata serves as a hub for commerce, culture, and technology. Positioned in this dynamic city, the company benefits from its access to a robust business ecosystem, a rich talent pool, and a thriving infrastructure. Kolkata's diverse industries, coupled with its increasing focus on digital transformation, create an ideal environment for fostering innovation and driving growth. This prime location allows the company to tap into local and global markets, ensuring that it remains at the forefront of emerging trends and technologies.
Kolkata is well-positioned to become a leading hub for business analytics, driven by its growing digital infrastructure, expanding talent pool, and vibrant business ecosystem. The city’s diverse industries, from manufacturing to finance and healthcare, stand to benefit immensely from the adoption of data-driven decision-making. By overcoming challenges related to talent, data security, and infrastructure, Kolkata can harness the full potential of business analytics to foster economic growth, enhance operational efficiencies, and support innovation across various sectors. As Kolkata embraces the future of business analytics, it is poised to become a key player in India’s data-driven economy.
